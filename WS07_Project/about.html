Hi! I'm Nemo Hirvelä, and I made this online store to sell products that I make myself. The products are not kept in stock but instead, they are made to order. All products are made from 100% recycled materials.

Please ask us any questions you have about our products or services, so we can create a FAQ for our future customers.

Welcome to our little corner of handcrafted creativity. By placing an order, you agree to the following simple terms: Each product is individually made to order by Nemo Hirvelä using 100% recycled materials. Because every item is handcrafted, small variations, marks, and imperfections are part of the product’s unique character and story. Production begins after an order is placed, so delivery times may vary depending on workload, material availability, and inspiration levels. We do our best to ship every order as quickly as possible. Please treat all products with care and respect for the materials they were reborn from. Colors, textures, and details may differ slightly from product photos. If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 14 days and we’ll make things right. By supporting this store, you support sustainable creation, second chances for materials, and independent craftsmanship. Thank you for being part of the journey.
